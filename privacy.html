Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 31, 2026

1. Data Collection Principles

FLIXO processes minimal data strictly required for performance tracking. This includes Discord User IDs, Voice Channel IDs, and Guild configuration matrices. We strictly do not log user data, personal logs, text message contexts, or internet protocol addresses.

2. Processing & Storage

Data caching occurs in active runtime memory to preserve playlist pipelines. Session history caches are instantly and permanently purged the millisecond the application ends its voice stream channel configuration or is removed from a guild.

3. Third Party Disclosures

We do not sell, trade, monetize, or distribute data layers to external systems or networks. Data access endpoints are closed securely inside our hosting environment infrastructure layers.
